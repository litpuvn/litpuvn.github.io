TING - Text, Interpretation, Network, and Generative AI Lab

Long Nguyen, Ph.D.

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of Louisville. Previously, I was an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Data Science at Meharry Medical College. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science at Texas Tech University, United States. I had worked over 15 years in software industry in different countries before turning to academia career. My research interests include data mining, artificial intelligence, and machine learning, with special interests in TING aspects - Text, Interpretation, Network, and Generative AI. Some specific areas are including but not limited to Foundation Models, Large Language Models, Natural Language Processing, Time-series Analysis and Anomaly Detection. I enjoy advancing AI techniques and turning science to benefit community at large.

Research Interest:

Generative AI, Deep Learning, Responsible AI/ML, Natural Language Processing, Representation & Interpretable methods, Learning on Graphs
